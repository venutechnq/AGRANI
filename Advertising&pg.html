Why Choose Photography

Learn different skills

There are a lot of different roles in an advertising agency. Agencies hire Accounts Managers, Graphic designers, Content Writers, and Directors, etc. You can don different hats or choose to specialize in one area. The choice is yours.

Quick Promotions and faster career growth

In advertising, age is just a number. Even an intern who has lots of creative ideas and potential will quickly rise through the ranks. Hard work and exceptional skills are rewarded in this industry. Traditional hierarchy is non-existent in this field.

Work with the biggest brands

You might like the sound of a Ferrari, or you might love downing pint after pint of Corona. If you work in an agency, you will get to work directly with big brands. Apart from meeting your favorite idols, you get to know what makes a brand tick and what are their core values.

Win awards for your ideas

In India, there are a lot of festivals that recognize the advertising community's contribution to society. Events like The Goa fest, Ink Spell, Fox Club, and Kyoorious regularly gives awards to agencies that do exceptional work. Creative Thinks Media is one such agency that has won many awards for running exceptional campaigns and for proving excellent services to their clients..
