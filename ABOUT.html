ABOUT AGRANI

WELCOME TO AGRANI

Agrani colleges has its operational base at Vijayawada. Agrani Educational Group has signed a MOU with mutliple Universities for conducting Hotel Management, Tourism & Catering technology programme through Regular/Distance Education Mode throughout the globe.

THE MISSION STATEMENT

To Provide Career-oriented Education & Training in Business Administration ,Information Technology, Hotel Management, Catering and Tourism & Event Management Disciplines in an inspiring atmosphere of independent thinking and innovation to create multi-skilled professionals and future managers with rewarding Careers in professional world.

TRUST VISION

Agrani has a Contemplative Aspiration for its Acclaimed Global Presence in the field of Professional Education.

CHERISHED VALUES

Agrani strives to inculcate Leadership Skills, to Build-up a Reservoir of Knowledge, Goals to bring out the Hidden Potentials and also Moulds the young minds to keep tempo with the Changing Economic Development.
