Why Choose Photography

Versatile industry

The scope of fashion designing is just not restricted to clothing; rather, many other work profiles combine and contribute to creating stunning designs. It involves the work of jewellery designers, textile designers, fashion merchandisers, influencers and much more.

Opportunities abroad

Many reputed brands such as Donatella Versace, Ralph Lauren, Coco Chanel, and Kate Spade are always looking for skilled designers to serve their clients.

Diversity

Making a career in the fashion industry allows you to work with a diverse clientele with different needs and requirements. This allows fashion designers to explore their core strengths, especially at the beginner level.

TYPES OF DESIGNS

Fashion Design
  • Different labs for pattern making, draping, construction, etc. help students to gain practical exposure of the complete fashion designing process.
  • Equipped with large number of soft boards and mannequins.
  • Through mannequins, they learn about positioning and pinning fabric to carve out the design
  • Industry-relevant tools and software help students to design patterns for garment formation
Textile Design
  • Fully Equipped handloom and weaving labs with variety of yarns, threads, looms & upgraded machinery.
  • Equipped with high-end analytical instruments used in the process of textile testing.
  • Students learn the process of weaving and different testing parameters to maintain quality of material.
Fashion Events
  • Global Fashion and Design Festival provide students a platform to exhibit their talent and creativity in front of industry experts through their designs
  • The festival invites fashion designers, models and renowned personalities who help students to gain exposure of the real industry environment.
Jewellery Design
  • The jewellery designer has to be very creative,artistic, technically sound, aware of the market trends and have business sense. Both hand drawing and drafting methods and computer aided design programs are used in designing jewellery. Rapid development in technology and machinery has provided artists with easier alternatives to producing a variety of jewellery items.
