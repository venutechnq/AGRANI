ADMISSION PROCESS

Step1:Application Form

Application Form and Prospectus can be obtained from the Counselors in the campus from Monday to Saturday, between 10am to 6pm, upon paying the requisite fee.

The applicants also have the option to download Application Form for offline submission.

The applicants can fill up online registration form.

The duly filled Application Form then needs to be submitted along with the mandatory documents. For more information, go through the Documentation List.

Step2:Entrance Test & Interview Process

Agrani Admission process includes personal interview for selection in the interested academic program. On successful payment of registration fees, candidate will have to follow the given steps:

 Personal Interview:The Personal Interview or Telephonic/ Skype Interview (for outstation applicants) will be conducted.

Guidelines and time schedule will be duly provided to the applicant beforehand.

Step3:Result Declaration

The assessment report will be conveyed via applicantâ€™s registered email-id or SMS to given mobile number with the Admissions Department, within 3 days.

Step4:Admission Confirmation

Successful candidate will be provided with Admission Letter from Admissions Department confirming his/her admission to the opted program. Thereupon, the applicant is required to submit the Confirmation Fee (as per his/her Plan), as initially communicated by the Admissions Counselor.

Step5:Original Documents Submission

All Original Certificates/Documents, along with one set of attested true copies of the documents in support of their claim at the time of filling up of Application Form for Admission, will have to be presented to the Admissions Department at the time of the reporting for the scrutiny and verification. It is mandatory to get the Receipt-cum-Acknowledgement duly signed by the Admissions Department.

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Eligibility Criteria

All candidates applying for Bachelor programmes must fulfill the following criteria:

.Applications for Academic Year 2019-20 is open to applicants who have passed or will appear for the qualifying examinations under the higher secondary (10+2) from any recognized Board of Education such as CBSE/ICSE/IGCSE/IB and State Board, during the academic year 2018-19.

All candidates applying for Master programmes must fulfill the following criteria:

.Applicants must either have appeared or passed, or will be appearing in any of the following, before July 2019:
 Bachelors degree in any discipline under the 10+2+3 (or more) system, from any university or institute recognized (by law) in India.
 Diploma in related field of study under the 10+2+3 system, from any university or institute recognized (by law) in India.

Note: In case Degree/Certificate has been obtained from a University/Board of any other country, then an equivalence certificate must be obtained by the candidate from Association of Indian Universities (AIU), New Delhi, prior to admission.
